Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Byou, we collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: When you make a purchase, we collect your name, phone number, shipping address, and payment details.
  • Order Information: Details about the products you purchase, including size, color preferences, and order history.
  • Communication Data: Any correspondence when you contact us via WhatsApp, email, or social media.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders and inquiries
  • To provide customer support and address any issues
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send promotional offers (only with your explicit consent)

3. Information Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure storage of customer data with restricted access
  • Encryption of sensitive information during transmission
  • Regular security assessments of our systems

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Delivery services to fulfill your orders
  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
